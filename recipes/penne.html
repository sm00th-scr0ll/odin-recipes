Penne Pasta Bake


Description

Three-cheese chicken penne features chunks of chicken in a creamy tomato sauce with basil and fresh spinach baked with multigrain pasta. Serve with Crystal Light iced tea.

Ingredients

  • Cooking spray
  • 1 1/2 cups multigrain penne pasta
  • 1 (9 ounce) bag fresh spinach leaves
  • 1 pound boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil leaves
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can dried tomato, drained
  • 1 (14 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 2 ounces Philadelphia Neufchatel cheese, cubed
  • 1 cup Kraft 2% milk shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 2 tablespoons Kraft grated parmesean cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F. Grease a casserole dish with cooking spray.

  2. Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add penne> Cook, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 11 minutes; add spinach to boiling water for the last minute. Drain and set aside.

  3. Grease a large nonstick skillet with cooking spray and place over medium-high heat. Cook and stir chicken and basil in the hot skillet for 3 minutes. Stir in tomatoes and spaghetti sauce; bring to a boil. Simmer on low heat until chicken is cooked through, stirring occasionally, about 3 minutes. Stir in Neufch√¢tel cheese until melted.

  4. Add penne-spinach mixture and 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese to the skillet; stir until well combined. Pour into the prepared casserole dish.

  5. Bake in the preheated oven until heated through, about 20 minutes. Top with remaining 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese and Parmesan. Continue baking until mozzarella is melted, about 3 minutes.
